Musical Eras

Explore the rich history of music through its various eras.

Baroque Era

The Baroque era, spanning from approximately 1600 to 1750, is characterized by its ornate musical style and the use of counterpoint, with composers like Bach and Vivaldi.

Classical Era

The Classical era, from about 1750 to 1820, emphasizes clarity, balance, and form, with composers like Mozart and Haydn.

Romantic Era

The Romantic era, from the early 19th century to the early 20th century, is marked by emotional expression and individualism, with composers like Chopin and Wagner.

Modern Era

The Modern era, from the early 20th century to the present, is characterized by experimentation and a break from traditional forms, with composers like Stravinsky and Schoenberg.
